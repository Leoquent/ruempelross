Frequent questions

We've got answers

What does a clearance cost?

It depends on the size, how full the property is and the access. Small cellars start at around €130, flat clearances at around €1,170. After a free on-site visit you receive a binding fixed price – a possible value offset lowers the cost.

What happens if something gets damaged?

We are 100% insured (Gothaer). Should anything break during the work, we cover the damage in full.

How does the value offset work?

Well-kept furniture, collectibles or antiques are offset against the clearance price. That can lower your invoice significantly – in some cases even to zero.

Do you also clear hoarder flats?

Yes – discreetly, respectfully and without judgement. We have experience with difficult situations and treat personal belongings with the care they deserve.

How quickly can I get an appointment?

Thanks to short waiting times, usually within a few days. We reply to your request within 24 hours – often the same day.

Do you have an English-speaking team and work with expats & military (PCS) families?

Yes. Our team speaks English and we regularly help expats and US military families with PCS and move-out clearances – flexible at short notice, fixed price in writing, and a broom-clean handover your landlord will accept.
